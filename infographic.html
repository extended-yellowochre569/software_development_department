Collaboration Protocol
1
Question
Clarify requirements before proposing anything
2
Options
Present 2–4 directions with clear pros/cons
3
Decision
User chooses — always the final decision maker
4
Draft
Show draft or summary before writing files
5
Approval
"May I write this to [file]?" — confirmation required
